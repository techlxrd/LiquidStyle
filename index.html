LiquidWeb

Apple Liquid Glass for the web!

Usage

Add these to your <head>:
<link rel="stylesheet" href="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/techlxrd/LiquidWeb/liquidweb.css">
<script src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/techlxrd/LiquidWeb/liquidweb.js"></script>

Example

<div class="liquid-glass">Your content here</div>
